1. 100% / 200% Money Back Guarantee

We offer a Money Back Guarantee for our workshop. If you attend the live session and genuinely feel that the workshop did not provide value, you may request a refund within the specified refund period.

2. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • You must have purchased a valid workshop ticket.
  • You must attend the live workshop session.
  • You must submit your refund request within 48 hours after the workshop ends.
  • The refund request must include a genuine reason for dissatisfaction.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued if:

  • You fail to attend the live workshop.
  • The request is made after the refund window has expired.
  • Payment disputes or chargebacks are initiated without contacting us first.
